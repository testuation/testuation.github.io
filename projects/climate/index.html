MACA

  • January 1, 0001

Make climate great again

Promising new approaches to mitigate and adapt to climate change are emerging today from laboratories and startups. But on their current course, most will take too many decades to achieve the scale of adoption necessary to make a meaningful contribution to our urgent climate challenges. ActuateSM will explore programs to accelerate scale-up by experimenting across the many complex and interacting factors, from process technology and manufacturing to finance and policy, that determine the pace of expansion. These future ActuateSM programs would be designed to research and demonstrate pathways by which specific nascent approaches can achieve full-scale impact rapidly.
