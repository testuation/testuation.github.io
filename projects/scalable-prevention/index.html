Scalable Prevention of Chronic Disease

Program objective: research and develop automated tools for healthy habits and demonstrate their effectiveness in disease prevention.

The Scalable Prevention of Chronic Disease program takes aim at one of the most expensive and rapidly growing areas of U.S. healthcare: chronic disease. Despite a degree of progress with wellness and coaching programs, fewer than one percent of the 84 million Americans at risk of diabetes are pursuing the healthy habits that are proven to prevent the disease. New research in engagement, monitoring, coaching, and incentives is opening the door to highly automated, radically better behavior assessment and modification. Building on this emerging research, Actuate’s program aims to demonstrate a next generation of lifestyle management methods that are inexpensive enough to reach tens of millions of Americans and effective enough to help prevent millions of them from transitioning into chronic disease.
