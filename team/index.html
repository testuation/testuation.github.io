Who we are

Arati Prabhakar

CEO

Arati’s career has spanned multiple leadership roles as a public servant and in Silicon Valley, with the common thread of advancing research and technology to achieve societal and business impact. She was director of DARPA 2012-17 and director of NIST 1993-97. In between, she spent a decade as a partner at an early-stage venture capital firm.

After WWII, America built the most effective R&D capacity that the world had ever seen. Today our challenges are different, our opportunities are different, and our global context is different. It’s time to renew R&D’s promise of a better tomorrow.

Wade Shen

Program Manager

Wade is a data science and artificial intelligence expert who has funded and worked with researchers in universities and companies, users in governments and in companies, and policy makers to create and demonstrate solutions to thorny problems. He was a program manager at DARPA 2014-19. Before that, he conducted and managed R&D at MIT Lincoln Laboratory and founded a startup company.

‘We can do so much more today with the new advances from research. [add]’

Carolyn Shaw

Director of Operations

Carolyn worked with program managers and the leaders at DARPA 2013-18 to make things happen. While getting her MBA she reunited with Arati and Wade to start Actuate.
