We envision a generational advance in the US R&D ecosystem that significantly boosts our ability to address complex, critical challenges.

To accelerate this essential advance, ActuateSM will design and conduct applied integrative R&D programs that demonstrate breakthrough solutions for today’s challenges.

Our program approach: define bold objective and program plan, tap the best talent in universities, companies, nonprofits, conduct applied integrative R&D, and conclude with a convincing demonstration of a breakthrough capability and a community ready to advance, implement, and scale.
