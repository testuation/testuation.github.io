Applied R&D for 21st century challenges

The ultimate aim of ActuateSM is to significantly boost our society’s ability to innovate for the challenges we face for the future. ActuateSM will conduct a portfolio of research programs that can open powerful new options to meet essential societal needs and serve as models for effective new forms of innovation.

Our goal

Applied R&D for societal challenges

New types of data and analytical and experimental techniques, methods to model and reason about complexity, and deep domain expertise can now be combined to form hypotheses, to design effective experiments, and to much more deeply understand and influence complex systems from biology to weather to behavior and social dynamics. Actuate’s work is to research and demonstrate fundamentally new approaches to complex societal problems. As they unfold, these programs and methods will model new forms of innovation to help prevail over the challenges ahead.
