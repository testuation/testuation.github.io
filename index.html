Applied R&D for 21st century challenges

The ultimate aim of ActuateSM is to significantly boost our society’s ability to innovate for the challenges we face for the future. A nonprofit organization that conducts applied research for societal challenges, ActuateSM will construct a portfolio of programs that can open powerful new options to meet essential societal needs. In doing so, these research programs can serve as models for effective new forms of innovation. ActuateSM will also chart new directions for the U.S. innovation ecosystem so that it can address our complex challenges in the decades ahead.
