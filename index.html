Breakthroughs for society’s challenges

Opportunity and good health for every person. Information we can trust. Thriving even as the climate changes. We can’t wait.

We can envision a future with robust research and innovation that creates powerful new solutions for society’s complex, critical problems. Our mission is to enable this future faster. We do that by researching and demonstrating breakthroughs for societal challenges - to show how a new generation of innovation can help shape the future.

The US innovation ecosystem is a phenomenal engine for meeting challenges identified in the last century. Now we have new, unmet challenges: economic opportunity for every person, better health at a cost that doesn’t cripple the economy, data and information that individuals can trust, and mitigation of climate instability. Each is critical to our success as a society. We don’t yet know how we will solve any of these challenges. Innovation is limited or incomplete here with neither the market nor established government agencies addressing these goals. But the good news is that new ideas and fresh hypotheses are brewing today. They allow us to imagine how we can make great strides of progress.
