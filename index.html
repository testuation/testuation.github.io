Breakthroughs for society’s challenges

To accelerate a generational advance in the US R&D ecosystem, Actuate, a nonprofit, will design and conduct applied, integrative R&D programs that demonstrate breakthrough solutions for today’s challenges.

Discover Our Programs

Our goal

Innovation for our future

The US innovation ecosystem is a phenomenal engine for meeting challenges identified in the last century. Now we have new, unmet challenges: economic opportunity for every person; better health at a cost that doesn’t cripple the economy; data and information that individuals can trust; and mitigation of climate instability. Actuate will conduct a portfolio of programs to address these problems.
