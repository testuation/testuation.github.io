DataSafes

Program objective: research and demonstrate statistical modeling and machine learning across multiple datasets with rigorous privacy protection.

Actuate’s Data and Privacy program aims to integrate and demonstrate advanced research from math, cryptography, statistics, and computer science to start a new chapter in practical data privacy in which data is analyzable and private at the same time. The program will advance and integrate these new privacy technologies in an end-to-end architecture and will test the system on a series of social science research challenges. The resulting capability offers the prospect of resolving one of the great quandaries of our time: personal information is the most valuable data for addressing many societal challenges and simultaneously the most dangerous data for individual privacy.
