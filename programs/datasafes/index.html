DataSafes

AI and statistical model of data while protecting privacy

Private data could hold the solution to some of the most difficult societal problems, but strong privacy restrictions limit their use in research.

Radical data sharing enabled by privacy-preserving AI and statistics

DataSafes is an end-to-end system for analyzing datasets owned by different parties. This effort builds on research advances in differential privacy, secure computation and automated record linkage, and automated statistical modeling.

If successful, DataSafes will enable:

  1. analysis of multiple datasets without having to establish mutual agreement or trust among their owners
  2. privacy controls for data owners and individuals that is automatically managed
