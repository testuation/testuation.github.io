Make climate great again

Program objective: explore acceleration opportunities and develop programs that are systems experiments in accelerating scale up of promising climate solutions.

Promising new approaches to mitigate and adapt to climate change are emerging today from laboratories and startups. But on their current course, most will take too many decades to achieve the scale of adoption necessary to make a meaningful contribution to our urgent climate challenges. Actuate will explore programs to accelerate scale-up by experimenting across the many complex and interacting factors, from process technology and manufacturing to finance and policy, that determine the pace of expansion. These future Actuate SM programs would be designed to research and demonstrate pathways by which specific nascent approaches can achieve full-scale impact rapidly.
